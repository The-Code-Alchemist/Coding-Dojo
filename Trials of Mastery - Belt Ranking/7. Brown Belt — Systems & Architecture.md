# 🟤 Brown Belt — Systems & Architecture

![Brown Belt](../assets/brown-belt.png)

---

## 🧠 Overview

Welcome to the Brown Belt.

At this stage, you move beyond single applications and begin to think in terms of **systems and architecture**.

You are no longer building isolated services —
you are designing systems that must **work together, scale, and evolve**.

---

## 🧠 Architect Mindset

At Brown Belt, you no longer think in features.

You think in systems.

* “Where does this responsibility belong?”
* “What happens if this service fails?”
* “How do these services scale independently?”
* “What are the trade-offs of this design?”

You are designing systems that must evolve over time.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

After completing the Brown Belt, you will be able to:

* Design and structure a **multi-service system**
* Define clear **service boundaries**
* Implement **communication between services**
* Evaluate **architectural trade-offs**
* Understand **scalability and system limitations**

---

## 🧰 Required Setup

You must have completed:

* All previous belts up to Purple

---

## 📚 Topics Covered

* Monolith vs Microservices
* Service communication (REST)
* Synchronous vs asynchronous communication
* API design
* System boundaries & domain separation
* Coupling vs cohesion
* Basic scalability concepts
* Fault tolerance (intro)

---

## 🧪 Assignments

---

### Assignment 1 — Split Your Application

* Break your application into multiple services
* Define clear responsibilities
* Justify **why** you split it this way

---

### Assignment 2 — Service Communication

* Implement communication between services (REST)
* Handle request/response flows
* Handle **failures between services** (timeouts, retries)

---

### Assignment 3 — System Design

* Design your system architecture
* Create a diagram of your services
* Clearly define service boundaries

---

### Assignment 4 — Identify Trade-offs

* Monolith vs microservices
* Explain your design decisions
* Identify trade-offs and consequences

---

### Assignment 5 — Documentation

* Document your system design
* Explain interactions between services
* Describe responsibilities per service

---

### Assignment 6 — Failure & Scaling Scenario

* Simulate a service failure
* Explain system behavior
* Describe how your system could scale

---

## ⚔️ Trial of Mastery — Brown Belt

To earn your Brown Belt, you must:

* Design and implement a **multi-service system**
* Demonstrate **reliable communication between services**
* Provide a clear **architecture diagram**
* Explain **system boundaries and responsibilities**
* Identify and justify **architectural trade-offs**
* Explain how your system handles **failures**

You must be able to answer:

* “Why did you split your system this way?”
* “What happens if one service fails?”
* “How would your system scale?”

---

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes

* Splitting services without clear boundaries
* Tight coupling between services
* Ignoring failure scenarios
* Overengineering simple problems
* Not understanding trade-offs

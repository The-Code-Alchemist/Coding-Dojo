# For Companies

The Coding Dojo is increasingly used by companies
as a baseline for assessing and upskilling software engineers.

## Why companies use this Dojo

- Predictable engineering quality
- Shared technical language across teams
- Reduced onboarding time
- Fewer build and deployment issues

Rather than training for specific frameworks,
the Dojo focuses on transferable fundamentals.

## Use cases

- Internal developer upskilling
- Baseline assessment for new hires
- Alignment across consultancy or delivery teams
- Technical due diligence

## What companies get

- Clear insight into engineering maturity
- Practical, code-based evaluation
- A reusable internal reference standard
